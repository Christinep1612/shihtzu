    • Introduction

    Thank you for your interest in IbelieveinShihtzu.com

    I Believe in Shih Tzu was created out of love for my 3 rescued Shih Tzus and my passion for dog training. With this blog, my goal is to not only share my passion with others, but to also create a platform where pet owners, training professionals, and pet product companies can come together with the singular goal of   fostering positive human-canine relationships. In addition to being a proud dog mommy, I am an accomplished small dog pet trainer, Canine Good Citizen evaluator, and pet photographer.  I only use positive reinforcement methods and do not believe in the use of e-collars, prong collars, or any other device which inflicts force, pain, or fear upon a dog while training.

    Along the way, I will share stories and musings about my whole four-footed family. In addition to the 3 Shih Tzus, I am the proud mommy of Kyra, a Norwegian Elkhound mix adopted from Best Friends of Las Vegas; Daisy, an energetic Boston Terrier; and Sunny, a rescued snow dog of origins unknown. I also am owned by two loving cats – Ninja and Baby Kitty.

    Guidelines

    I believe integrity and honesty is essential to create lasting relationships. As such, I will only promote or provide advertising opportunities to those products and services that I would use with my own dogs.

    As a positive reinforcement trainer I will not accept advertising opportunities with companies that promote, manufacture, or sell items such as e-collars, prong collars, or any other device which inflicts force, pain, or fear upon a dog while training.  Also, when comes to pet edibles, I well on promote products that are either manufactured in the USA or otherwise have impeccable credentials for quality and food safety standards.
